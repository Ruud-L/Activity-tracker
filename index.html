Activity Tracker

ACTIVITY TRACKER

A fully local app for tracking activity and measuring productivity.

Every X minutes, a lightweight popup asks what you're doing.

You can also set an activity manually and only confirm it every Y minutes. All timings and many other options can be adjusted in the app settings.

Available languages: see list below.

How It Works

Track your workflow across the day and review your history clearly.

Prompt-Based Tracking

A lightweight popup asks what you're doing every X minutes, so tracking stays easy and consistent.

Calendar History

See your past activities in a calendar view and understand what you actually worked on over time.

Statistics Overview

All tracked data is summarized in statistics, so you can spot patterns and improve your productivity.
